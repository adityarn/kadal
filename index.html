Introduction

This blog talks about the fascinating science of the Earth Systems that determine the climate of our planet – the oceans, atmosphere, cryosphere, terrestrial processes, and the biosphere. Every month, a new scientific article is discussed here in an approachable language that everyone can understand! I am always on the lookout for new collaborators who would like to contribute to this blog, please contact me if you too are a climate geek and want to make the science more accessible to a wide audience.
